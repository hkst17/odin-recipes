Sandra's Enchiladas

Description

These enchiladas are perfect for people who love cheesy flavors! It is comforting and hearty.

Ingredients

  • ½ Onion
  • 1 pound Ground Meat
  • 1 packet Taco Seasoning
  • 6 cups Cheese
  • 6 Tortillas
  • 1 can Enchilada Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix Enchilada Sauce Recipe together, then heat to incorporate.
  2. Brown the Meat (don’t overcook) and throw out the excess liquid. Fry Onions in the same pan. Add the Taco Seasoning.
  3. Prepare Tortillas on a plate. Add some Cheese and Meat to the middle of the Tortilla. Wrap!
  4. Pour some Enchilada Sauce on the bottom of the storage pan. Place wrapped enchiladas in the pan and pour more Enchilada Sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle Cheese all over enchiladas and bake at 350 degrees for 20 mins or until Cheese melts.
