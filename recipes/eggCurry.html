Sandra's Sour Egg Curry

Description

This Egg Curry is perfect for people who love tangy flavors! It is refreshing, umami, and hearty.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Paprika
  • Fish Sauce
  • Tamarind Powder
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs so they become hard boiled eggs.
  2. Add oil to a hot pan.
  3. When oil is hot, add in the onions and cook until almost transparent.
  4. Add in the tamarind powder, fish sauce, and paprika. Stir.
  5. When fragrant, add in the tomatoes until they soften then simmer.
  6. Peel the eggs and cut in half.
  7. When the sauce becomes a stew, add in the sliced up eggs and simmer for another 3 mins. NOTE: It is okay if some of the yolk goes in! It just makes it creamier and the sauce thicker.
