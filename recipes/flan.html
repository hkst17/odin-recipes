Sandra's Flan

Description

This flan recipe is perfect for people who love sweet flavors! It is creamy and melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • Â¼ cup Erythritol
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Egg Yolk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix everything together (remember to put 2 Eggs and ONE YOLK).
  3. Add mixture to two separate bowls to cook evenly.
  4. Add bowls to a boiling water bath and put in the oven.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 mins.
